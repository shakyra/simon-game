Inside the Heart and Mind of Kurt Cobain

    A Snapshot of Kurt Cobain's Life:

  • February 20, 1967 Born in Aberdeen, WA
  • July 1985 Arrested for spray painting buildings with his friends
  • June 15, 1989 Nirvana's debut studio album, Bleach, is released
  • January 12, 1990 Cobain meets Courtney Love in Portland's Satyricon Nightclub
  • February 24, 1992 Cobain and Love are married on Waikiki Beach in Hawaii
  • August 18, 1992 Frances Bean Cobain is born
  • March 3, 1994 Cobain is hospitalized due to overdose from champagne and Rohypnol
  • March 25, 1994 An unsuccessful intervention is attempted
  • March 30, 1994 Cobain agrees to attend inpatient rehab at the Exodus Recovery Center in Los Angeles
  • April 8, 1994 Age 27, Cobain's body was discovered at his Lake Washington Boulevard home by electrician Gary Smith
  • April 5, 1994 The day Cobain was estimated to have died
